Privacy Policy — The UI Generator

DRAFT — version draft-2026-07-23. This document has not yet been reviewed by counsel and will change before commercial launch.

What we collect

The minimum to run accounts and saved work: your email address, your saved design document, and a record of the terms version you accepted (with its date and your browser language). Passwords are handled entirely by Supabase Auth — we never see or store them. No payment details exist in this phase; when payments launch they will be handled by Stripe, and card details will never touch our database.

What we do with it

Operate your account, store and sync your work, secure the service, and respond to your requests. That's the list.

What we don't do

We do not sell personal information. There is no advertising, no tracking pixels, and no analytics that capture your design content. Your projects are private by default and are never published or showcased without your explicit opt-in.

Who processes data for us

Supabase (authentication and database) and GitHub Pages (static hosting). Future subprocessors (e.g. Stripe for payments, an email provider) will be listed here before they are used.

Minors

The service requires users to be 13 or older, affirmed by a neutral checkbox at account creation. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Your controls

Download your complete saved document any time (account menu → Download my data). Sign out keeps your work on your device. Account and data deletion requests are honored — contact the operator; deleting your account removes your profile, saved document, and consent records.

Changes

This policy will change before launch (counsel review, subprocessor list, contact address). Material changes will be versioned and re-consented where required.
